Discover UMD

Why I Chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because of the Labovitz School of Business and Economics, and because of the beautiful location of the campus. UMD has a strong business program that aligns with my career goals, and the management major that I am pursuig. The campus is located right by Lake Superior, providing stunning views and outdoor activities.

What You Like About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

My favorite thing about Duluth is Lake Superior and the nature surrounding it. The Lake is special, and being able to live near such a vast body of freshwater is amazing. I also love to mountain bike and snowboard, and Duluth is home to some of the best mountain biking in the world, and amazing snowy winters. Duluth has a vibrant community and plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy year-round.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

Some of my favorite personal experiences that I have made at UMD are creating the Ski and Snowboard Club, and the Mountain Biking Club. Creating these two clubs with my friends have allowed me to meet many new people, and gain skills that will help me in the future. My favorite part about campus is that everything is connected indoors. This means that in the winter, I can walk from class to class without having to go outside in the cold!
