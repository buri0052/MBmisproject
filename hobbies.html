My Hobbies

Mountain Biking

Mountain Biking has been a major part of my life since I was 14 years old. I have competed in numerous races and have been able to travel to amazing places. I have become better and better every season, and hope to continue to grow in this sport. My favorite place to mountain bike in Duluth is Spirit Mountain. I ride a Kona Operator, and a Transition Sentinel.

Snowboarding

Snowboarding has made a huge impact on my life. Since a very young age, I have always enjoyed snowboarding. I use snowboarding as an escape, and it has always helped me clear my mind and enjoy the outdoors during the winter months. It has also helped me land a job at Vail Resorts as a Terrain Park Attendant, where I get to spend my winters maintaining the terrain parks. I love the community that is built around snowboarding, and I would not be myself without it. I ride a Sleep Snowboards White Elephant and an Evol Vessle.

Personal Stories and Experiences

I have had the privledge of being a captain of my High School Mountain Biking Team, and now an offcier of the UMD Mountain Biking Club. I have learned valuable lessons through these experiences that have helped me learn how to be a leader, and helped me learn who I am as a person.

Why are Mountain Biking and Snowboarding Important To Me

Mountain Biking has been a passion for me since I was 14. I raced Mountain Bikes for my High School team, and I have now started the UMD Mountain Biking Club with my friends. I have found a love for being outdoors, and it is a great ways to meet new people. Snowboarding has always been a way for me To clear my head. I have always found that being outside, even in the cold, makes me hapier and brings up my mood! I would not be the Personal I am today without Snowboarding.
